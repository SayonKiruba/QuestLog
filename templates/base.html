Quick help

Do I need an account?

You can browse Discover and search for games without signing in. Create a free account to save games to your library, edit your profile, and use Profiles.

How do I add a game?

Search from Discover, pick a title, then add it to your shelf with a status, optional rating, and notes when you are logged in.

Where does game information come from?

Cover art and details are provided by IGDB.

Where is my library and profile?

Use Library for your personal shelf and Profiles for community profiles (sign in required).
